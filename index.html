05 · France — three-pillar system

FRANCE: THREE REGULATORS,
ONE COMPLIANCE RECEIPT.

No single national AI law exists. France enforces AI obligations through three concurrent frameworks — each with its own authority, enforcement power, and audit expectations.

🇪🇺
PILLAR 1

EU AI Act

Directly applicable. 4 risk tiers, conformity assessments, technical documentation, human oversight. Art. 50 active Aug 2.

🔒
PILLAR 2

GDPR / RGPD

CNIL enforces. Art. 22 right to object to automated profiling. DPIAs required. Right to erasure creates Machine Unlearning obligations.

🇫🇷
PILLAR 3 · SREN LAW

Loi SREN · May 2024

Reinforces ARCOM + CNIL powers. Transparency obligations for generative AI accessible in France — certificates showing what AI created what content.
